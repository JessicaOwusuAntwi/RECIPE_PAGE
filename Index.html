My favourite Meal

Jollof rice is my favourite meal.

ABOUT

Jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa. The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat in a single pot, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions.

INGREDIENTS

INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Onions, roughly chopped Two large Ones
Vegetable Oil One-Third cup (80 ml), plus two teaspoons
Diced Tomato Fourteen Oz(395 g), 2 cans
Tomato Paste Six oz(170 g), 1 can
Habanero pepper One
Curry powder Two teaspoons
Garlic Powder One teaspoon
Ground Ginger One teaspoon
Mixed Dried Herbs Half teaspoon
chicken bouillon cubes, crushed Three
long grain rice(500 g), rinsed Two and Half cups
frozen mixed vegetable(150 g) One cup
Water(360 mL One and Half cups

PREPARATION

  1. Add onions and 2 tablespoons of oil to a blender and pulse until smooth. Transfer to a medium bowl.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and habanero pepper to the blender, and pulse until smooth. Transfer to a separate medium bowl.
  3. Heat the remaining â…“ cup (80 ml) of oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
  4. Once the oil is shimmering, add the onion puree and cook until the water has cooked out and the puree is starting to brown, about 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree and add the curry powder, garlic powder, ginger, dried herbs, and crushed bouillon cubes. Cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the stew has reduced by half and is deep red in color
  6. Add the rice, mixed vegetables, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with foil and a lid. Simmer for another 30 minutes, until the rice is cooked through and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Enjoy!
